    • Rules

    §1 - Discord

    §1.1 - Discord TOS
    The Discord TOS must be followed at all times. A breach of the TOS will lead to a ban from the server and a report to the Discord moderation team.

    §1.2 - Family Friendly
    To have a wonderful community, please keep the server safe and family-friendly so all are welcome in our server!

    §1.3 - Pinging
    Please avoid pinging higher roles, as they are often busy or engaged in important tasks. If you need assistance, kindly open a ticket in the "Tickets" section of our Discord server, or click on "Contact" in the top bar. Alternatively, you can reach out directly by clicking here.

    §1.4 - Swearing
    To keep our community server family-friendly, we need to communicate respectfully and kindly.

    §1.5 - Leaking
    Do not leak any sneak-peeks or information about our games/upcoming games or anything else unless you have permission to do so.

    §1.6 - Spam
    Spamming messages, images, or reactions is not allowed. Please keep the conversation meaningful.

    §1.7 - Personal Information
    Do not share personal information (yours or others’) in the server. Privacy is important for all members.

    §1.8 - Promotion
    No self-promotion or advertising of any kind is allowed without prior permission from the server staff.

    §1.9 - Respect Staff Decisions
    Always respect the decisions of the server staff. If you have a concern, address it politely and privately.

    §1.10 - Content Sharing
    Ensure that any content shared is appropriate and follows community guidelines. No NSFW or inappropriate material is allowed.

    §2 - Ingame

    §2.1 - Selling
    Do not sell any in-game items for Robux or real currency.

    §2.2 - Respect Others
    Treat all players with respect. Harassment, bullying, or hate speech will not be tolerated.

    §2.3 - Cheating and Exploiting
    Using cheats, hacks, or exploits to gain an unfair advantage is strictly prohibited.

    §2.4 - Inappropriate Content
    Do not post or share any inappropriate content, including but not limited to offensive language, images, or links.

    §2.5 - Teaming
    Players should not team up in competitive modes unless the game specifically allows it.

    §2.6 - AFK Players
    Avoid using automated scripts or being AFK (Away From Keyboard) excessively in active gameplay areas.

    §2.7 - Game Bugs
    If you discover a bug or glitch, report it to the game developers rather than exploiting it for personal gain.

    §2.8 - Account Sharing
    Do not share your account information with others. Account sharing can lead to bans or losses.

    §2.9 - Scamming
    Scamming players is strictly prohibited. All trades must be fair and transparent.

    §2.10 - Chat Restrictions
    Use appropriate language in the game chat. Spamming or excessive self-promotion is not allowed.

    §2.11 - Reporting Issues
    If you experience any issues with other players or the game, please report them to the moderators.

    §2.12 - Game-Specific Mechanics
    Follow any additional rules related to specific game mechanics, as outlined in the game's instructions or description.

    §2.13 - Event Participation
    Players participating in events must follow the event-specific guidelines and rules.
